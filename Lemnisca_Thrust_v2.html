Why scale-up stalls

Pilot success doesn't guarantee industrial confidence.

Your pilot may be running. The milestone is real — a CDMO transfer, demo campaign, or commercial launch. But four hidden problems quietly compound, and most teams don't see them until the next expensive batch is already running.

No clear diagnosis

Your batches vary — some good, some bad. But you don't know when they diverge or what drives it. Every week spent guessing is a week without an answer.

Batch trajectory overlay Divergence window

Scale is a black box

What works at pilot doesn't automatically work at 100,000 litres. Mixing, OTR, CO₂ stripping, and feed gradients all shift between scales — and failures discovered late cost 10–100× more to fix.

Your data is sitting idle

Years of batch records, time-series trends, and deviation logs exist — but aren't being analysed together. Millions in R&D spend, generating zero forward momentum.

Every wasted week is market share lost.

While you're stuck in scale-up cycles, your competitor is moving. Each delay pushes your launch further out, compounds your losses, and hands the market to someone else — with millions spent and nothing to show for it.
